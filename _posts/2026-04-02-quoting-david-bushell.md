> Generative AI is art. It's irredeemably shit art; end of conversation. A child's crayon doodle is also lacking refined artistry but we hang it on our fridge because a human made it and **that matters.** We care and caring has a positive effect on our lives. When you pass human creativity through the slop wringer, or just prompt an incantation, the result is continvoucly morged; a vapid mockery of the input. The garbage out no longer matters, nobody cares, nobody benefits.

-- David Bushell, [I quit. The clankers won.](https://dbushell.com/2026/04/01/i-quit-the-clankers-won/)
